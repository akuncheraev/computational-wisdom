Computational Wisdom

Master the computational tools to understand the world and rule the machines.

Our Service

We teach in tiny groups of intrinsically motivated students who are typically 12-17 years old and stand out with their skills among their peers. We find suitable teammates for our students and provide them with guidance, consultancy and leadership in local, national and international projects and competitions.

Our areas of expertise include: Core tools of computational wisdom; namely algebras and algebraic reasoning, logics and logical reasoning, probabilities and probabilistic reasoning, statistics and statistical reasoning, geometries and geometric reasoning, algorithms and algorithmic reasoning... In addition to these, programming skills and web, mobile and game development using these skills... We teach computer science in general, and focus on artificial intelligence and machine learning.

We are based in Izmir, Turkey. However, we serve to all world via online meetings.

Our Methodology

Instead of following a fixed curriculum, we progress in programs that we adapt in line with the interests and potential of our students, regardless of their grade. We work not on exam-oriented knowledge but on problem-solving skills that focus on real-world applications, and we encourage critical thinking. We believe in the power of the master-apprentice relationship, and communication and teamworkskills.

We are actively working on building AI-powered tools and scalable platforms to accelerate the process and widen the scope. Our latest works include Dersilyon and Code Literacy.

Our Team

Our team consists of idealist educators who not only have pedagogical knowledge and experience, but also have both international academic studies and publications, and sector experience in engineering and entrepreneurship, who also have devoted their lives to education and have in-depth knowledge of their subjects.

Teaching is our greatest passion. We see that potential talents in many children and teens are wasted due to absence of guidance or presence of misguidance. We are wishing to raise great scientists, engineers, entrepreneurs; geniuses who can understand and shape the world

Be Our Student!

Our teaching techniques are sometimes unusual and partially experimental. For a limited time, our service is free of charge. For using our resources efficiently, however, we are very selective with our students. Contact us for requesting more information or applying for the program. You can find our contact address if you inspect the source code of this page. We don't have a typical application form as we don't offer a typical service.
